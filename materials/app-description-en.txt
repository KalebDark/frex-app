With Frex you can use your mobile or tablet to easily create fascinating fractal images. Generating fractals is a compelling and relaxing exercise! You can select from various fractal types and then discover the beauty of their inner details. You can change their colors and further enrich the images by various options.

Once you are satisfied with your work you can set it as your personal wallpaper, send it to your friends, or copy it into your gallery.

Frex is free software.

Enjoy!
